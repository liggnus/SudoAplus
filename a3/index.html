Group Processes

In Assignment 2, the group maintained a consistent meeting schedule despite the challenges posed by differing time zones and personal schedules. Members generally attended meetings regularly or had valid reasons for absences. The tasks for the assignment were divided among group members based on their skills, previous contributions, and workload. Dividing work in an efficient manner allowed for all members to contribute an equal amount to the final result. Therefore, our group managed time effectively throughout the period we worked on Assignment 2. We believe we can finish with a quality product in assignment 3.

In Assignment 3, our group has implemented multiple changes in order to improve our efficiency further. Knowing that this project will have to be larger than the previous one we had worked on together, we have implemented a project management tool contained within Microsoft Teams. After the group reflected on a lack of leadership and direction in Assignment 2, Katherine volunteered to take on a leadership role and therefore was the member who assigned tasks appropriately. By utilizing the scheduling function in MS Teams, Katherine created a timeline for tasks to ensure that the group finished the assignment on time. By utilizing the scheduling function and having members familiar with relevant technology, the group believe assignment 3 can finish more effectively and efficiently towards the end goal.

But one great commonality for each member is the possibilities that completion of their studies will open up for the future.
