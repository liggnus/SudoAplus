Jobs and Skills

Our project needs four leading roles to develop it to the next phase. There are Back-end Developer, UI/UX Designer, Full Stack Engineer and Scrum Master .

On each role's responsibilities and Required Qualifications and work experience are:

  1. Back-end Developer:

    We are seeking a highly motivated and skilled Back-end Developer to join our team and help take our weather and climate data website for farmers to the next phase. As the Back-end Developer, he will be responsible for building and maintaining the server-side architecture of the website, ensuring that it is scalable, secure, and efficient.

    Some of Key Responsibilities are:

    • Design and implement the back-end architecture of the website, including the database, API, and server-side logic.
    • Ensure that the website meets all security and performance standards and that data is stored and processed efficiently.
    • Develop and maintain automated tests to ensure the reliability and stability of the website.
    • Stay up-to-date with current technologies, frameworks, and best practices, and incorporate them into the design and implementation of the website.

    Required Qualifications and work experiences are:

    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
    • 3+ years of experience as a Back-end Developer with a strong portfolio of server-side projects.
    • Strong knowledge of back-end technologies, such as Node.js, Ruby on Rails, Python.
    • Strong understanding of database technologies, such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Cassandra.
    • Experience with server-side security, performance optimization, and scalability.

  2. UI/UX Designer:

    We seek a highly motivated and talented UI/UX Designer to join our team and help take our weather and climate data website for farmers to the next level. As the UI/UX Designer, he will be responsible for creating and implementing the website's overall look, feel, and user experience, including the design of all interfaces, data visualizations, and user flows.

    Some of the Key Responsibilities are:

    • Work with cross-functional teams, including developers, meteorologists, and product managers, to understand the requirements and constraints of the project.
    • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups to communicate design concepts and user flows.
    • Conduct user research and usability testing to validate design decisions and ensure that the website meets the needs and expectations of farmers.
    • Create and maintain a design system that ensures consistency and scalability of the user experience.
    • Collaborate with the development team to ensure that the design is implemented correctly and that the website meets all technical requirements.
    • Stay up-to-date with current design trends, technologies, and best practices, and incorporate them into the design of the website.

    Required Qualifications and work experience are:

    • Bachelor's degree in UX Design, Graphic Design, HCI, or a related field.
    • 3+ years of experience as a UI/UX Designer, with a portfolio that showcases his work on data-driven websites and applications.
    • Strong knowledge of design tools and software, such as Sketch, Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and HTML/CSS.
    • Passion for design and a commitment to delivering exceptional user experiences.

  3. Full Stack Engineer:

    We seek a Full Stack Engineer to take our weather and climate data website to the next phase. The ideal candidate will have a solid technical background with experience developing and deploying scalable, secure web applications. In this role, he will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining the website's front-end and back-end systems.

    Some of Key Responsibilities are:

    • Design and implement scalable and secure front-end and back-end systems for the weather and climate data website.
    • Work closely with the UI/UX Designer and Back-end Developer to ensure a seamless user experience.
    • Develop and maintain the website's database, ensuring data integrity and security.
    • Stay up-to-date with new technologies and trends, and recommend improvements to enhance the website's functionality.

    Require Qualifications and work experiences are:

    • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
    • At least 5 years of experience in full-stack development.
    • Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and at least one front-end framework (e.g., React, Angular, Vue.js).
    • Experience with at least one back-end programming language (e.g., Node.js, Python, Ruby on Rails).
    • Experience with databases, such as MySQL, MongoDB, or PostgreSQL.
    • Familiarity with DevOps tools and practices, such as Docker, Kubernetes, and continuous integration and delivery.
    • Excellent problem-solving skills, with the ability to find creative solutions to complex problems.
    • A passion for innovation and a drive to continuously improve the user experience.

  4. Scrum Master:

    We seek a skilled and experienced Scrum Master to join our team and take the weather and climate data website project to the next phase. The ideal candidate should have a deep understanding of Agile methodologies, with experience leading cross-functional teams through successful product delivery.

    Some of Key Responsibilities are:

    • Led the implementation of the Scrum framework, ensuring that all team members understood and followed Agile best practices
    • Facilitate daily stand-up meetings, sprint planning, retrospectives, and demos.
    • Act as a mediator between the development team, stakeholders, and management to resolve conflicts and ensure alignment
    • Ensure the team's adherence to the Definition of Done and identify areas for improvement in the process.
    • Provide coaching and mentoring to the development team to promote continuous learning and improvement.

    • Bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, or a related field
    • At least 5 years of experience as a Scrum Master on software development projects
    • Certified Scrum Master (CSM) certification or equivalent
    • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to effectively communicate with team members, stakeholders, and management
    • Strong leadership and facilitation skills, with experience leading cross-functional teams and managing stakeholder expectations
    • Proven track record of delivering high-quality software products using Agile methodologies
